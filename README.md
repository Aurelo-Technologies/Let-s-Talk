## 🚀 About Us

At Aurelo Technologies, we merge **software engineering**, **AI**, and **digital experience design** to create solutions that empower brands, teams, and organizations to operate smarter and faster.  

We develop, manage, and deploy systems that power creative media, event technology, automation, and digital transformation for enterprises and ministries.

## 💡 What We Build

- **AI Systems & Automation:** Smart agents, process optimization tools, and ML-driven analytics.  
- **Web & Cloud Engineering:** Robust web apps, APIs, and microservices built with modern stacks (Flask, FastAPI, Django, React, and Next.js).  
- **Media & Event Tech Solutions:** Tools for live visual experiences, content automation, and multimedia integration.  
- **Platform Development:** Custom dashboards, CMS systems, and enterprise SaaS platforms.  
- **Music & Creative Infrastructure:** Technical systems behind Aurelo Mediaworks, powering digital music distribution, artist management, and marketing pipelines.
